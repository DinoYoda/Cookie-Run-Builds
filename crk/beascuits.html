Beascuits

How Beascuits Work

Beascuits provide four additional stat lines and are a major part of endgame optimization.

Like toppings, quality is determined by both line type and roll strength, not just rarity.

General Priorities

  • Meet survivability and cooldown needs first.
  • Then stack damage stats that match the cookie's role and team.
  • Element-focused teams should prioritize the matching elemental line once core breakpoints are met.

Practical Notes

If a build card lists specific beascuit targets, those values take priority over generic recommendations.

For most accounts, one strong all-purpose beascuit per cookie is better than several half-finished niche variants.
