Info & Build Guide

Reading Builds

Each cookie page lists one or more recommended builds. A build shows the topping set, beascuit, and any target stat thresholds you should aim for.

Builds marked Best are the primary recommendation. Recommended builds are solid alternatives. Unlabelled builds are situational or experimental.

Toppings

Toppings grant flat stat bonuses and, when five of the same type are equipped, a set bonus. Resonance toppings swap the set bonus for a unique resonance effect tied to a specific cookie.

Substat priority is listed in order on each build. Focus on the first listed substat first.

Beascuits

Beascuits provide four additional stat slots. Tainted beascuits have a special element-damage slot in position 1 replaced with a second passive slot.

Some cookies have different stats in each slot but for the most part you can't go wrong with a beascuit full of the cookies preferred stat or elemental DMG.

Stats

Below is a quick reference for what each stat does. CRIT Resist, Debuff Resist, and DEF are usually not worth building — prioritize other stats first.

HP — Health or Health Points is the amount of damage a Cookie can take. If their HP reaches 0, they will be defeated.
ATK — Attack is used to calculate how much damage a Cookie (or enemy) does. It is currently unknown how this damage is calculated.
DEF — Defense is used to calculate how much received damage to a Cookie or enemy is reduced by a flat amount. The higher their DEF, the less damage they receive.
CRIT Chance — Critical Hit Chance (CRIT% or CRIT Rate) determines the probability of an attack to critically hit and deal critical damage. This stat has a cap of 100%.
Cooldown — CD is the amount of time before a Cookie can use their skill. Cooldown Reduction cannot exceed 60%.

Advanced Stats

ATK SPD — Attack Speed determines how much faster a Cookie or enemy can attack compared to normal. It affects Regular Attacks and Skills unless noted otherwise. ATK SPD-increasing buffs are capped at 500%, and ATK SPD cannot be reduced by more than 70%.
DMG Resist — Damage Resist or Damage Reduction reduces damage received by a percentage. Cap: 90%.
CRIT Resist — Critical Damage Resist reduces only critical damage received by a percentage. Cap: 85%.
Debuff Resist — Determines how less affected a Cookie or enemy is to debuffs, reducing effect and/or duration. Cap: 75%.
Amplify Buff — Amplified Buffs determines how much a buff's effect and/or duration is increased. Cap: 75%.

Stat Targets

Where a build lists a stat target (e.g. Cooldown ≥ 15%) it is the minimum threshold needed for the build to function as intended. Going above the cap for a stat is generally wasted.

Common caps to be aware of:

  • Cooldown — 50% total reduction (70% with Watch Treasure at max level)
  • DMG Resist — no hard cap, but diminishing returns apply in most content

Rating

Each cookie has a rating assigned to it. This is an overall rating of the cookies performance across all game modes and a sort recommendation on who to prioritize building. Because of this a cookie that is essential for certain game modes but not very good in others may recieve a poor rank. For this reason I'd suggest using the tier lists to determine which cookies to build for specific game modes instead of taking the rating as gospel.

Build Ranking

Builds are given a yellow or pink thumbs up to determine rank. Yellow = Best and Pink = Recommended. I assume that if you're checking out builds for a certain cookie you want to build them so all have a best (in my opion and from my research) build that you'll want. Recommended however means I'd recommend building this in addition to the best build for that cookie. Unranked builds can be worth building depending on you situation but are mostly there to showcase different wasy the cookie ahs been built.
