Toppings

Toppings are gear pieces that increase a Cookie's stats. Cookies unlock topping slots as they level, up to a total of 5 slots.

Each topping gives a main stat based on its type, and can gain bonus substats as it is upgraded. Toppings are upgraded with topping pieces and coins.

At higher upgrade levels, toppings roll extra bonus lines. Substat quality matters just as much as the topping type for final performance.

Resonant Toppings

Resonant toppings can only be equipped by certain cookies, usually thematic with the topping, but have higher minimum substat values. They also only offer the 6 meta substats: ATK, ATK SPD, CRIT%, Cooldown, DMG Resist, and CRIT Resist.

That last one is why resonant toppings are not strictly better than normal ones. HP is used by many cookies and CRIT Resist none. I can only pray Devsis changes this one day. I think Ancient Root toppings on my Millenial Tree would look really nice.

Topping Tarts

Topping Tarts are separate gear pieces that boost the same stat family as toppings. A cookie can equip 1 tart at a time.

Unlike toppings, tarts only provide a primary stat value and do not roll random bonus substats.
