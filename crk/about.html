About & Guide Hub

Reading Builds

Each cookie page lists one or more recommended builds. A build shows the topping set, beascuit, and any target stat thresholds you should aim for.

Builds marked Best are the primary recommended build. Recommended builds should also be built but perhaps with less priority than the best build. Unlabelled builds are situational or experimental.

Sets lists all the topping sets and beascuits I found the cookie to have used in my research no matter how niche. Check it out if you want some ideas but the builds should cover everything themselves.

Review & Rating

Each cookie has a review written by yours truly explaining my thoughts on the cookie and roughly explaining use cases. These are limited by my own knowledge of the game and my own experiences with the cookie so treat them as such. A subjective thing added to give the site some more flair.

Each cookie has a rating assigned to it. This is an overall rating of the cookie's performance across all game modes and a recommendation on who to prioritize building first.

Because of this some cookies which are mandatory or at least very good in certain game modes can have a lower overall rating so use the tier lists to determine usefulness in any given game mode and don't take the rating too seriously.

Build Ranking

Builds use yellow and pink thumbs up as rank markers. Yellow = Best, Pink = Recommended.

I assume when looking at a cookie's build information you intend to build that cookie so each cookie has a best build that I'd say you should/must have is you're going to build that cookie.

Recommended builds should also be made but more situational as not every cookie has one.

Unranked builds can still be useful for specific matchups or account needs, and are included to show viable or just interesting alternatives.

Assumptions & Baseline

Rankings and build recommendations are made using a high-investment baseline so results are comparable.

  • Cookies and skills are evaluated as max level.
  • Builds assume good to near-perfect toppings and strong beascuit lines.
