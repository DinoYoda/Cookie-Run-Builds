Stats

Base Stats

HP

Health or Health Points (known as HP) is the amount of damage a Cookie can take. If their HP reaches 0, they will be defeated.

ATK

Attack (known as ATK) is used to calculate how much damage a Cookie (or enemy) does. It is currently unknown how this damage is calculated.

DEF

Defense (known as DEF) is used to calculate how much received damage to a Cookie or enemy is reduced by a flat amount. The higher their DEF, the less damage they receive.

CRIT Chance

Critical Hit Chance (known as CRIT% or CRIT Chance/Rate) determines the probability of an attack to critically hit and deal critical damage. This stat has a cap of 100%.

Cooldown

Cooldown (or CD) is the amount of time it takes before a Cookie is able to use their skill. In terms of Cooldown Reduction, it cannot be reduced by more than 60%.

Advanced Stats

ATK SPD

Attack Speed (ATK SPD) determines how much faster a Cookie or enemy attacks compared to their normal speed. It affects regular attacks and skills unless a skill says otherwise. ATK SPD buff effects are capped at 500%, and ATK SPD reduction is capped at 70%.

DMG Resist

Damage Resist (or Damage Reduction) lowers incoming damage by a percentage. This stat is capped at 90%.

CRIT Resist

Critical Damage Resist (or Critical Damage Reduction) lowers only critical hit damage taken by a percentage. This stat is capped at 85%.

Debuff Resist

Debuff Resist reduces how strongly debuffs affect a Cookie or enemy, including effect strength and/or duration depending on the debuff. This stat is capped at 75%.

Amplify Buff

Amplify Buff (or Amplified Buffs) increases the strength and/or duration of buffs applied to a Cookie or enemy. This stat is capped at 75%.
