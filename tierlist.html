What the tiers mean
S+

A team with these Cookies could probably clear every level in the game—they're the best all-around choices in the roster.

S

Cookies in this tier are effective, powerful, and valuable in many places in the game, often being either highly versatile in their use cases or excellent counters to specific enemies.

A+ & A

These are overall strong Cookies that can be used as substitutes for others, or are essential for specific team compositions.

B

B-tier Cookies are useful for early-game and/or niche activities. They're passable at what they do.

C & D

Cookies in these tiers are either outdated or simply can't pull their weight. Any unique features they have are unlikely to be worth investing in.

E

E-tier Cookies are largely ineffective and offer no real benefit when used. Please consider higher-ranking Cookies instead.
